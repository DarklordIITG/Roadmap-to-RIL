Your Preparation Runway from Year 2

Phase 1: Groundwork (Year 2)

This initial phase is about building a rock-solid foundation. You will master core chemical engineering principles, begin developing crucial software skills, and understand the opportunity you are targeting. This is where your 18-month strategic advantage begins.

Phase 2: Acceleration (Year 3)

Now you build on your foundation to create a standout profile. This phase is about tackling advanced subjects, translating skills into high-impact projects, gaining a competitive edge, and methodically preparing for every stage of the rigorous selection process.

This final phase focuses on the end goals: effectively networking to gain insights and making the most of the internship to earn a Pre-Placement Offer (PPO). Learn what to expect on-site and how to demonstrate the key attributes of a future RIL leader.

Strategic Networking Playbook

Intern to PPO: The Conversion Strategy

  • ✔
    Demonstrate Technical Competence

    Deliver a high-quality, technically sound project by applying your academic knowledge and software skills.

  • ✔
    Show Learning Agility & Curiosity

    Ask intelligent questions, learn quickly, and show genuine interest in processes beyond your project's scope.

  • ✔
    Embrace an Ownership Mindset

    Take full responsibility for your project. Proactively identify next steps and anticipate potential challenges.

  • ✔
    Master Teamwork & Interpersonal Skills

    Build positive rapport with mentors, co-interns, and plant operators. Respect their experience and listen actively.

  • ✔
    Deliver a Powerful Final Presentation

    Your final presentation is the capstone. It must be data-driven, logically structured, and provide clear, actionable recommendations.
